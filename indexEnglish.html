NXT museum

Welcome to the NXT museum

History

Nxt Museum, founded by the young entrepreneur Merel van Helsdingen, exhibits large-scale art installations, that are developed from interdisciplinary collaborations between established and emerging artists, designers, technicians, musicians and scientists.

Reviews:

The NXT Museum is a gem for anyone fascinated by the world of technology. The compact but comprehensive exhibitions offer a journey through the evolution of robotics and artificial intelligence. The hands-on displays provide an engaging experience, making complex concepts accessible to everyone. Whether you're a tech enthusiast or just curious about the future, this museum is a must-visit. Knowledgeable staff and a well-curated collection make it a wonderful destination for a technology-inspired day out.

The NXT Museum offers the perfect balance between education and entertainment. It's a futuristic playground for both children and adults, with interactive exhibits showcasing the latest in robotics. The museum's user-friendly layout seamlessly guides visitors through the evolution of NXT technology. Informative yet accessible, it's an excellent destination for families and technology enthusiasts alike. A visit to the NXT Museum guarantees a day full of learning and fun!
